English

Last updated: April 27, 2026

MioCam ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Camera and microphone (live monitoring)

The App uses your device's camera and microphone to stream real-time video and audio for monitoring. This live media is sent directly between your devices (peer-to-peer) using encrypted communication. We do not record, store, or upload your live monitoring video or audio to our own servers for playback or archiving.

2. Account and cloud data (Firebase)

The App uses Google Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication (e.g. Sign in with Apple) and Cloud Firestore, to operate pairing, sessions, and related features. Data stored may include your account identifier, display name if you provide one, camera/monitor linkage, connection/session metadata, and similar information needed to run the service. This is separate from live monitoring media in Section 1. For how Google processes data, see Google's Privacy Policy and Google's documentation for Firebase.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App may display banner advertisements (for example on the Monitor live screen), delivered through Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK. Advertising partners may use identifiers, usage-related data, approximate location, diagnostics, and similar information for advertising and measurement, as described in Google's policies. Where required, a consent form (Google User Messaging Platform) may be shown; where applicable, App Tracking Transparency may be requested. See Google's Privacy Policy and Google's information for partners and users.

4. Other third-party services

The App may rely on Apple's standard platform services (e.g. Sign in with Apple, push notifications) under Apple's terms and privacy policy.

5. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: itoguchi.app@gmail.com
